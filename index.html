Phaneroo Make Manifest

Phaneroo Ministries International

Make Manifest · Founded 2014 · Kampala, Uganda

A dynamic, life-transforming and generational impacting ministry — raising sons of God, separated for the demonstration of the God-life to the world and crowned with the glories of eternity.

Vision
Transform Nations
To transform nations and the entire world with the Word of God — raising a generation of believers who walk in the full knowledge and manifestation of the Word of God.
Mission
Make Manifest
To reveal the power of God in the lives of believers — equipping them with biblical teachings that empower victorious Christian lives and positive influence in society.
The Name
"Phaneroo"
From the Greek — meaning "to make manifest." Bringing to visibility that which existed but was not seen. Christians demonstrating the God-life to the world.
Outreach
Nations & Campuses
Active in 200+ educational institutions in Uganda through Manifest Fellowship, and weekly outreach in over 15 prisons across the country.
